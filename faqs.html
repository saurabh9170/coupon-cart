Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I sell a gift card? Upload your card, set a price, and get paid when it’s sold.
  • How long does it take to get cashback? Cashback is usually credited within 24–72 hours after successful purchase.
  • Can I cancel a transaction? Some transactions can be canceled before they are finalized. Contact support for help.
  • Is my payment secure? Yes, all transactions are encrypted and processed securely through verified channels.
  • Do gift cards expire? Most cards have an expiry date, always check before buying or selling.
